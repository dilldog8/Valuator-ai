FAQ

Common questions

Does the AI replace the valuer?

No. Valuator AI produces a draft report and supporting data. A licensed valuer always reviews, edits and signs off before anything is sent to a client or institution.

What types of assets can it value?

We're launching across the categories professional valuers already cover — property, plant & machinery, art, antiques & jewellery, specialised assets like yachts and aircraft, agricultural land, and financial/business valuations — with more added based on waitlist demand.

How is the report compliant with industry standards?

Reports are structured around recognised valuation methodology (in line with standards such as RICS, SAIV and SAPOA), including comparable evidence and condition notes, so they fit existing institutional review processes.
